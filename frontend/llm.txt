# ENAULD - LLM Context File

ENAULD Technology Health Research and Services is a Netherlands-registered consultancy established in November 2010. It provides research, monitoring, evaluation and learning services to the health sector, with an integrated technology component added in October 2021.

Core service areas:

- Health, Research and Services: impact evaluation, theory-based and mixed-methods evaluation, MEL system design, evidence synthesis, capacity building, knowledge translation and primary data collection.
- Information, Management and Technology: information systems design, data management strategy and technology integration for monitoring, evaluation and research programmes.

The company is led by Dr. Ngozi Akwataghibe, MD, MPH, PhD, Principal Consultant and Senior Evaluation Specialist. Her work spans global health, public health, impact evaluation, implementation research, policy analysis, WASH, health systems strengthening, health financing, gender and equity analysis.

Selected client and partner experience includes the World Bank, UNICEF, WFP, WHO, Gavi, 3ie, the Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation, Shell and national governments.
